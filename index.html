Owen Ward

Bio

I am a PhD candidate and research assistant in the Department of Spanish and Portuguese at the University of Toronto. My dissertation investigates how speakers of Spanish and speakers of Portuguese differ in their perception and production of English lexical stress. My aim with this project is to investigate the link between segments and phonological processes affecting the production of segments in the L1 with the acquisition of suprasegmental features in an L2.
